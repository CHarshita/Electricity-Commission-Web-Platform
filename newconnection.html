Getting a Domestic Connection

Thank you for choosing to engage with the largest Public Sector Undertaking in the electricity sector and for giving us the opportunity to serve you. We kindly request that you carefully review the instructions before filling out the application form.

Click here for Registration

Submit Your Application Apply online. Fill out the application form and submit the required documents: ✔ Proof of Identity (Aadhar, PAN, Voter ID) ✔ Proof of Address (Ration Card, Property Tax Receipt) ✔ Ownership Document or No Objection Certificate (NOC) from the landlord (if rented) ✔ Load requirement details

Inspection & Approval Our team will inspect your premises to check the feasibility of the connection. Upon approval, you will receive a confirmation.

Payment of Charges Make the required payments online or offline: Security Deposit Connection Charges Service Line Cost (if applicable)

Once the payment is received, we will install the electricity meter and activate your connection. ✅ Your home is now powered with electricity!

Getting a Non-Domestic Connection

Thank you for choosing to engage with the largest Public Sector Undertaking in the electricity sector and for giving us the opportunity to serve you. We kindly request that you carefully review the instructions before filling out the application form.

Click here for Registration

Application Submission Apply online through the official portal. Fill out the non-domestic electricity connection form and submit required documents: ✔ Business Registration Certificate (if applicable) ✔ Proof of Identity (Aadhar, PAN, Company Registration, etc.) ✔ Proof of Address (Property Tax, Rent Agreement, etc.) ✔ Load Requirement Details & Electrical Layout (if required)

Inspection & Feasibility Assessment A technical team will inspect the premises to assess the feasibility of the connection. The inspection ensures compliance with safety regulations and verifies the required electrical load. Upon approval, an official confirmation will be sent via SMS, email, or notice

Pay the necessary charges as per the sanctioned load and connection type, including: Security Deposit Connection Charges Service Line Charges (if applicable) Payment can be made online through card and QR code

Meter Installation & Power Supply Activation After payment confirmation, the department will install the electricity meter and connect the service line. Once installed, the power supply will be activated, and the connection details will be shared with the applicant. ✅ Your commercial/non-domestic premises are now powered with electricity!
